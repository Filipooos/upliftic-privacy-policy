Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Summary. Upliftic does not require an account and does not display ads, track you across apps or websites, or sell personal data. Your calculation inputs and WhiteBoard notes remain on your device. The app sends limited app-launch information to Expo EAS Insights as described below.

Information stored on your device

Upliftic stores app settings and the values needed to restore your previous session. These may include fuel values, taxi-fuel settings, the density conversion factor, wind and runway inputs, WhiteBoard text, display and auto-dismiss preferences, the last edited field, and whether you accepted the in-app disclaimer.

This information is stored locally on your device. Upliftic does not transmit your calculation inputs, settings, or WhiteBoard text to the developer or to Expo. You can remove locally stored data by resetting available fields or by deleting the app from your device.

Limited app-usage information

When the app launches, the Expo EAS Insights service automatically receives a launch event together with a randomly generated app installation identifier, the Upliftic project identifier, app version, operating-system version, and platform. As with any network request, Expo may also receive technical network information such as an IP address.

This limited information is used to understand basic app usage and reliability. It does not include your name, email address, contacts, location, calculation inputs, or WhiteBoard text, and it is not used by Upliftic for advertising or cross-app tracking. Expo processes this information as a service provider. For more information, see Expo's Privacy Policy.

Information we do not collect

  • No name, email address, phone number, or other contact details
  • No account credentials
  • No precise or approximate location
  • No contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or health information
  • No advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data
  • No calculation inputs or free-form WhiteBoard content

Platform services

Apple or Google may process store, purchase, device, crash, or diagnostic information according to your platform settings and their own privacy policies. This processing is controlled by the platform provider, not by Upliftic.

Data retention and deletion

Upliftic does not keep your calculation inputs or WhiteBoard content on developer-operated servers. Locally stored data remains on your device until you reset it or delete the app. Information processed by Expo is retained according to Expo's policies. You may contact us about a privacy or deletion request using the address below.

Children's privacy

Upliftic is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect names, contact information, or user-entered content from children.

Your rights and contact

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding personal data, including access, correction, or deletion. To ask a question or make a privacy request, email kokpit250@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If Upliftic's data practices change, this policy will be updated and a new effective date will be posted on this page.
