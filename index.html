Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Summary. Upliftic does not collect, transmit, track, sell, or share personal data. The app works offline, requires no account, and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your calculation inputs, settings, and WhiteBoard notes remain on your device.

Information stored on your device

Upliftic stores app settings and the values needed to restore your previous session. These may include fuel values, taxi-fuel settings, the density conversion factor, wind and runway inputs, WhiteBoard text, display and auto-dismiss preferences, the last edited field, and whether you accepted the in-app disclaimer.

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or to any third party. You can remove locally stored data by resetting available fields or by deleting the app from your device.

Information we do not collect

  • No name, email address, phone number, or other contact details
  • No account credentials
  • No device, installation, or advertising identifiers
  • No precise or approximate location
  • No contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or health information
  • No usage analytics, diagnostics, or app-launch events
  • No advertising or cross-app tracking data
  • No calculation inputs or free-form WhiteBoard content

Platform services

Apple or Google may process store, purchase, device, crash, or diagnostic information according to your platform settings and their own privacy policies. This processing is controlled by the platform provider, not by Upliftic.

Data retention and deletion

Upliftic does not store personal data on developer-operated or third-party servers. Locally stored data remains on your device until you reset it or delete the app. Because Upliftic does not collect personal data, there is no server-side user data for the developer to retain or delete. You may contact us about any privacy question using the address below.

Children's privacy

Upliftic is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children or anyone else.

Your rights and contact

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding personal data, including access, correction, or deletion. To ask a question or make a privacy request, email kokpit250@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If Upliftic's data practices change, this policy will be updated and a new effective date will be posted on this page.
